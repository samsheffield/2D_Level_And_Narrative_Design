HOMEWORK

HOMEWORK: Wunderkammer

A wunderkammer, or cabinet of curiosities, were collections of rare, valuable, historically important or unusual objects, which were compiled by a single person for study and/or entertainment.

Create a small collection of objects which, when explored using only mouse input, reveal a story about their owner or a space.

Technical requirements:

  1. 2D
  2. Use of mouse only (no gamepad, no keyboard)
  3. Minimum of two scenes, with one persistent gameobject (singleton pattern + do not destroy)
  4. Use of World Space UI
  5. Use of the Animator

Some possible inspiration:

  • Gone Home and Its Hidden Objects by Elizabeth Goins (link)
  • 17th Century Still Life Painting in Europe (link)
  • Dana Newmann's Surrealist Cabinets of Wonder (link)
  • Solid Objects by Virginia Woolf (link)
  • The Museum of Jurassic Technology in LA (link
  • An Ode To Objects by Oscar Strik ( link)
  • Why the Food in 'Final Fantasy XV' Is So Damn Realistic by Whitney Reynolds (link)
