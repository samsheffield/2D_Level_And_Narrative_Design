HOMEWORK

HOMEWORK: POINTING

Create a small point & click game inspired by a news article.

  • This can be from a contemporary or historical, but it can not be fiction (no "fake news" either)
  • Minimum of 2 scenes (including Start scene)
  • Combine the point and click movement from this week's demo with the world UI from last week
  • Please include a link to the article which inspired your game, somewhere in-game

Good Luck!
