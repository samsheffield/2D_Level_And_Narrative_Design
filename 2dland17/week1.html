WARM UP "GAUNTLET" (DUE NEXT WEEK)

Create a small 2D game prototype based on some aspect of your winter break. The thematic details, goals, and mechanics are up to you. Keep it brief & manage your time!

Technical requirements:

  • Built in 2D using Unity
  • Switch between two scenes
  • Canvas-based UI
  • Input through Input Manager or GetKey
  • Rigidbody2D collision/trigger
  • Use of AudioSource (triggered sound or background audio)
  • Use of IEnumerator coroutine or InvokeRepeating
  • Exported build for your OS of choice

We will play these games at the beginning of class next week on your computer. Please create a tested build which can run on your computer. Bring a controller if necessary.

I will send everyone a link to their class Drive this week. Please put a copy your Unity files and build there.

Good Luck!
